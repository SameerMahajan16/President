Early Life & Education

Jimmy Carter was born on October 1, 1924, in Plains, Georgia. His parents were Earl Carter, a farmer and businessman, and Lillian Carter, a nurse. He had a modest upbringing and three siblings.

He graduated from the United States Naval Academy in 1946 and served in the Navy before returning to work on the family farm.

Growing up in the segregated South influenced his views on equality and justice.
