Life After Presidency

After leaving office, Jimmy Carter focused on humanitarian efforts. He founded the Carter Center, promoting peace, democracy, and public health.

He worked with Habitat for Humanity and received the Nobel Peace Prize in 2002 for his peace and mediation efforts.

As of 2025, Carter is 100 years old and remains committed to global health and peace, despite being in hospice care.
